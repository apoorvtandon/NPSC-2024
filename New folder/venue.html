About IIT INDORE

Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IIT Indore) is an Institute of national importance located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India. Established in 2009, IIT Indore has rapidly ascended in India's academic landscape. Internationally, IIT Indore was ranked 454 in the QS World University Rankings, 2023. In India, it was ranked 14 among engineering colleges by the National Institutional Ranking Framework in 2023. Research at IIT Indore covers a wide spectrum and is inspired by both real-world challenges and issues of fundamental importance. Additionally, the campus life at IIT Indore is dynamic and engaging, filled with a range of events and activities. Key highlights include the annual cultural festival Fluxus and the technical fest Avishkar.
