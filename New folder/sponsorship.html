Financial Sponsorship for NPSC 2024 are invited under following categories

Platinum Sponsor (Rs. 6 Lakhs):

  • The sponsoring organization will get complimentary registration for 5 delegates from the sponsoring organization.
  • Home page of 23th NPSC 2024 will display company logo along with hyperlink to company’s website.
  • Company logo on the banner of 23rd NPSC 2024.
  • Exhibition space.
  • Slide display of company’s name and logo in the start of each session.
  • Invitation for conference dinner to registered delegates.

    • Gold Sponsor (Rs. 4 Lakhs):

  • The sponsoring organization will get complimentary registration for 3 delegates from the sponsoring organization.
  • Home page of 23rd NPSC 2024 will display company logo along with hyperlink to company’s website.
  • Exhibition space.
  • Invitation for conference dinner to registered delegates.

    • Silver Sponsor (Rs. 3 Lakhs):

  • The sponsoring organization will get complimentary registration for 2 delegates from the sponsoring organization.
  • Home page of 23rd NPSC 2024 will display company logo along with hyperlink to company’s website.
  • Exhibition space.
  • Invitation for conference dinner to registered delegates.

    • Bronze Sponsor (Rs. 2 Lakhs):

  • The sponsoring organization will get complimentary registration for 1 delegates from the sponsoring organization.
  • Home page of 23rd NPSC 2024 will display company logo along with hyperlink to company’s website.
  • Exhibition space.
  • Invitation for conference dinner to registered delegates.
