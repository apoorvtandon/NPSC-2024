About 23rd NPSC-2024


The National Power System Conference (NPSC) has held its position as India's leading conference in power engineering since 1981. This biennial event serves as a platform for researchers, educators, and practitioners to share and discuss the latest innovations, trends, experiences, and concerns in electrical power and energy systems. NPSC brings together professionals from academia, industry, and utilities, fostering the exchange of ideas, knowledge, expertise, and experiences related to the evolving landscape of electric power systems. The theme of NPSC 2024 is: “Achieving Decarbonized, Digitalized Energy and Electric Transportation Systems”.
